Bangal Chicken Biriyani


Ingredients: 1. 8 pcs medium chicken. 2. 1 cup oil. 3. 500gm rice. 4. Ghee- 2 tbps. 5. Garlic- 10 pcs. 6. Ginger- 1 inch. 7. Curd - 1/2 cup. 8. Lemon Juice- 2 to 3 spoon 9. Green Chilli- 4. 10. Salt (as per taste) 11. Everest Biriyani Masala- 2 tbps. 12. Black Pepper- 1 teaspoon. 13. Chilli Powder- 1 teaspoon. 14. Soya Sauce- 3 teaspoon. 15. Tomato Sauce- 4 teaspoon. 16. Boiled Egg- 2. 17. Sugar- 2 tbps. 18. Cashew- 10 to 12 pcs.

Procedure: 1. Take ginger, garlic, 1 chopped onion and green chilli and make a paste. 2. Add the paste on the chicken along with the curd, lemon juice and salt. 3. Marinate the chicken for 5-7 hours. 4. Heat half cup of oil in a pan and fry the marinated chicken. 5. Then fry the marinated paste and then add soya sauce, tomato sauce, chilli powder, and black pepper. 6. Add the chicken and toss it. 7. Set the pan over medium-high heat and bring the water to a boil. 8. Take another pan put oil and ghee (1/2 cup), chop onions, salt, sugar, biriyani powder and 2tbps curd and mix properly. 9. Add rice in it and mix it properly. 10. Take a pan with boiled water (6-7 cups), add chicken and rice. Cook it on medium high heat, close the lid, so that the heat can not escape. 11. Lower the heat and let it cook for 20 min. 12. Then remove from heat and give 5-10 minutes standing time before serve. 13. Enjoy with raita and salad.
