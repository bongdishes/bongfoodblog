Bangal Chicken Biriyani


Ingredients:

  • 8 Pcs Medium Chicken
  • Basmati Rice- 500gm
  • Oil- 1 cup
  • Ghee- 2tbps
  • Ginger- 1 inch
  • Garlic
  • Curd- 1/2 cup>
  • Lime Juice- 2 to 3 spoon
  • Green Chiili- 4
  • Chilli Powder- 1tsp
  • Salt
  • Everest Biriyani Masala- 2tsp
  • Black Pepper- 1tsp
  • Soya Sauce- 3tsp
  • Tomato Sauce- 4tsp
  • Boiled Eggs- 2
  • Sugar- 2tbps
  • Cashew- 10 to 12 pcs

Procedure:

  1. Take ginger, garlic, 1 chopped onion, green chilli and make paste of it.
  2. Mix the paste in the curd, add some lime juice and then apply on the chicken
  3. Marinete the chicken for 5-7 hours
  4. Take a pan heat half cup oil and light fry the marinated chicken
  5. Light fry the marinated masala, add soya sauce, tomato sauce, chilli powder, black pepper and then toss it with the chicken.
  6. Take a pot and boil water
  7. Take a pan add oil and ghee (1/2 cup), chopped onions, salt, sugar, biriyani powder and curd- 2 spoon and mix it.
  8. Add basmati rice and mix it
  9. Take a big pan with rice and the chicken, add 6-7 cups of boiled water, close the lid and cook it on medium fire
  10. After some time (half boiled rice) add cashew and eggs, mix it and close the lid
  11. Switch off the gas
  12. Serve hot with raita and salad
